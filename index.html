About Alex Rodriguez

Baseball Legend

Alexander Emmanuel Rodriguez (born July 27, 1975), nicknamed "A-Rod", is an American former professional baseball shortstop and third baseman, businessman, and philanthropist.

A-Rod played 22 seasons in Major League Baseball (MLB) for the Seattle Mariners, Texas Rangers, and New York Yankees.

He is a 14-time All-Star, 3-time AL MVP, and won the 2009 World Series with the Yankees. He ranks fourth all-time in home runs (696) and RBIs (2,086).

After retiring, Rodriguez became a successful businessman and baseball analyst for ESPN and Fox Sports.
